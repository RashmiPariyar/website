Project 2: EcoMarket

EcoMarket is an eco-friendly e-commerce platform developed by Rashmi. With a focus on promoting sustainable living, EcoMarket offers a wide range of environmentally friendly products, from organic skincare to renewable energy solutions. Built using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB, EcoMarket features secure user authentication, a responsive design, and seamless integration with third-party payment gateways. Since its launch, EcoMarket has received positive feedback for its user-friendly interface and commitment to environmental sustainability.
